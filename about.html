Our Story

Auxano Schools was founded in 2010 with a vision to transform education in Nigeria by providing world-class learning experiences that nurture both academic excellence and character development. What began as a small educational institution has grown into one of Lagos's most respected schools.

Located in the vibrant community of Yaba, our school has been at the forefront of educational innovation, consistently adapting our curriculum and teaching methodologies to meet the evolving needs of our students and the global community.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students to become confident, creative, and compassionate leaders who make positive contributions to society. We strive to create an inclusive learning environment that celebrates diversity and fosters a love for lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Nigeria, recognized for academic excellence, innovative teaching methods, and the development of well-rounded individuals who are prepared to succeed in an ever-changing global landscape.

Our Core Values

The principles that guide everything we do at Auxano Schools

Excellence

We strive for excellence in all aspects of education, from academic performance to character development.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, ethics, and moral values in all our interactions.

Respect

We foster an environment of mutual respect, understanding, and appreciation for diversity.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and innovation in teaching methods and learning approaches.

Community

We build strong partnerships with families and the community to support student success.

Growth

We encourage continuous learning and personal growth for students, staff, and the institution.
