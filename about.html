Our Story

A journey of excellence in early childhood and primary education

Building Foundations for Success

AUXANO was founded in 2010 with a vision to transform early childhood and primary education in Yaba, Lagos. What began as a small educational institution has grown into a trusted name in quality education, serving hundreds of families in our community.

Our journey started with a simple yet powerful belief: every child deserves access to excellent education that nurtures their natural curiosity and prepares them for a bright future. Over the years, we have remained true to this vision while continuously evolving our programs and facilities to meet the changing needs of our students and families.

Today, AUXANO stands as a testament to our commitment to educational excellence, character development, and community service. We are proud to have helped shape the lives of countless children who have gone on to achieve great success in their academic and personal endeavors.

Mission & Vision

The principles that guide everything we do at AUXANO CRECHE, NURSERY AND PRIMARY SCHOOL

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing and stimulating learning environment that empowers children to develop their full potential academically, socially, emotionally, and physically. We are committed to fostering a love for learning, building strong character, and preparing students for future success.

Our Vision

To be the leading early childhood and primary educational institution in Yaba, Lagos, recognized for excellence in teaching, innovative learning approaches, and the development of well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.

Our Core Values

The fundamental principles that shape our educational approach

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, from academic achievement to character development and community service.

Care & Compassion

We treat every child with love, respect, and understanding, creating a safe and supportive environment for learning and growth.

Innovation

We embrace innovative teaching methods and technologies to enhance learning experiences and prepare students for the future.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, transparency, and ethical behavior in all our interactions and decisions.

Community

We foster a strong sense of community, encouraging collaboration, mutual respect, and support among students, parents, and staff.

Growth

We believe in continuous learning and personal development, encouraging both students and staff to grow and improve constantly.
