Our Story

Auxano Schools was founded in 2010 with a vision to transform education in Nigeria by providing world-class learning experiences that nurture both academic excellence and character development. What began as a small educational institution has grown into one of Lagos's most respected schools.

Located in the vibrant community of Yaba, our school has been at the forefront of educational innovation, consistently adapting our curriculum and teaching methodologies to meet the evolving needs of our students and the global community.

Our Mission & Vision

The principles that guide everything we do at Auxano Schools

Our Mission

To provide exceptional education that empowers students with knowledge, skills, and character to become responsible global citizens and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be the leading educational institution in Nigeria, recognized for academic excellence, innovation, and the development of well-rounded individuals who make positive contributions to society.

Our Core Values

The foundation of our educational philosophy

Excellence

We strive for excellence in everything we do, setting high standards for academic achievement and personal development.

Integrity

We uphold the highest standards of honesty, ethics, and moral character in all our interactions and decisions.

Respect

We foster a culture of mutual respect, understanding, and appreciation for diversity in our school community.

Innovation

We embrace creativity and innovation, encouraging students to think critically and solve problems creatively.

Service

We instill a sense of social responsibility and commitment to serving others and making a positive impact in the community.

Global Perspective

We prepare students for a globalized world, fostering cultural awareness and international understanding.
