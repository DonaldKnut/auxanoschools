Admissions

Admission Process

A simple and transparent process to join our educational community

1

Inquiry & Application

Submit an inquiry form and complete the online application with required documents.

2

Assessment

Students undergo age-appropriate assessments to determine their current academic level.

3

Interview

Family interview to discuss educational goals and ensure alignment with our values.

4

Acceptance

Successful applicants receive admission offers and enrollment instructions.

Admission Requirements

What you need to prepare for a successful application

Application Form

Completed application form with all required information.

Birth Certificate

Original birth certificate or certified copy.

Academic Records

Previous school records and transcripts (if applicable).

Medical Records

Immunization records and medical history.

Passport Photos

Recent passport-sized photographs of the student.

Application Fee

Non-refundable application fee payment.
