About Auxano Schools

Founded with a vision to provide exceptional education, Auxano Schools has been a cornerstone of academic excellence in Yaba, Lagos. Our commitment to nurturing young minds and building character has made us a trusted choice for parents seeking quality education for their children.

We believe that every child has unique potential waiting to be discovered. Our comprehensive approach to education ensures that students develop not only academically but also socially, emotionally, and physically.

Our Academic Programs

We offer a comprehensive range of academic programs designed to meet the diverse needs and interests of our students.

Early Years (Ages 2-5)

Foundation Stage

  • Play-based learning approach
  • Social and emotional development
  • Basic literacy and numeracy skills
  • Creative arts and physical activities

Primary School (Ages 6-11)

Key Stage 1 & 2

  • Core subjects: English, Math, Science
  • Social studies and humanities
  • Arts, music, and physical education
  • Character development programs

Secondary School (Ages 12-16)

Key Stage 3 & 4

  • IGCSE curriculum
  • Advanced subject specialization
  • Leadership and community service
  • Career guidance and counseling
