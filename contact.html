Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about Auxano Schools

What are the admission requirements?

Admission requirements vary by grade level. Generally, we require previous school records, birth certificate, and an entrance assessment. Please contact our admissions office for specific requirements for your child's grade level.

What is the student-teacher ratio?

We maintain a low student-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention and quality education for every student.

Do you offer scholarships?

Yes, we offer merit-based scholarships and financial aid programs for qualified students. Please contact our admissions office for more information about available scholarships.

What extracurricular activities do you offer?

We offer a wide range of extracurricular activities including sports, music, art, drama, debate, robotics, and various clubs. These activities help develop well-rounded individuals.

How do I schedule a campus visit?

You can schedule a campus visit by calling our office, filling out the contact form above, or emailing us. We offer guided tours on weekdays and some Saturdays.

What curriculum do you follow?

We follow the Nigerian National Curriculum enhanced with international best practices. Our curriculum is designed to prepare students for both local and international examinations.
